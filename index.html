Bringing smiles to hospice patients.

Dr. Dew Rite and Nurse Freckles, have been volunteering at CoxHealth since August, 2011. They have completed over 120 service hours on our oncology units, pediatric unit and various other patient units. They are professional compassionate, and very much appreciated by the patients, families and staff here at CoxHealth.
